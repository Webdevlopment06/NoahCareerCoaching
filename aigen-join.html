How to Join

Choose the path that best fits your goals. Whether you want to volunteer, receive coaching, or partner with us, we make the process clear and supportive.

Join as a Volunteer

Volunteers power our work. We seek committed individuals to support coaching, run workshops, and help with outreach.

  1. Apply: Submit a brief application so we can learn about your skills and availability.
  2. Orientation: Attend a short session to review responsibilities and program guidelines.
  3. Placement: Get matched to roles that suit your experience and interests.

Join as a Client

Our coaching supports job search, career transitions, and skill-building.

  1. Apply Online: Complete a short intake form to share your goals.
  2. Intake Meeting: Meet with our team to create a personalized plan.
  3. Begin Coaching: Access sessions, workshops, and resources tailored to you.

Apply Now

We typically respond within 3–5 business days.
